NeoN VJ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

Introduction

NeoN VJ (hereinafter "this app") is designed with user privacy as our top priority. This privacy policy explains what information this app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Microphone Usage

This app requires microphone access to provide music recognition functionality.

Purpose of Use:

  • Recognition of music playing in the surrounding environment (using ShazamKit API)
  • Real-time display of song information

Important Points:

  • Audio data is used solely for music recognition purposes
  • We do not record or store any audio
  • Audio data is processed only on the device and is not sent to external servers (except for music fingerprinting sent to ShazamKit API)

Data Storage

Local Data

This app stores the following settings only on your device:

  • Display settings (font size, layout, etc.)
  • Recognition settings (recognition mode, sensitivity, etc.)
  • Custom background images and logo images

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not sent to external servers
  • Is deleted when the app is uninstalled

Third-Party Services

Apple ShazamKit

This app uses Apple ShazamKit for music recognition. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to the use of ShazamKit.

  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

This app:

  • Does not sell personal information to third parties
  • Does not use data for advertising purposes
  • Does not share data without explicit user consent

Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as necessary. We will notify you within the app if there are any significant changes.
