Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used. NeoN VJ is designed with user privacy as a top priority.

Microphone Usage

The App requires access to the microphone to provide music recognition functionality.

Purpose of Use:

  • Recognition of music playing in the surrounding area (using ShazamKit API)
  • Real-time display of song information

Important Points:

  • Audio data is used only for music recognition
  • No audio recording or storage is performed
  • Audio data is processed only on the device and is not sent to external servers (except for music fingerprints sent to the ShazamKit API)

Data Storage

The App stores the following settings only on your device:

  • Display settings (font size, layout, etc.)
  • Recognition settings (recognition mode, sensitivity, etc.)
  • Custom background images and logo images

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not sent to external servers
  • Is deleted when the App is uninstalled
